Monterey Bay Academy Airport

Flight Operations

Pattern Altitude: 1,000 feet AGL for all traffic pattern operations.

Landing Direction: Left traffic pattern for all runways unless otherwise directed.

Safety Requirements

Pre-flight Inspection: Mandatory detailed inspection before each flight.

Weather Minimums: VFR conditions only - minimum 3 statute miles visibility.

Emergency Procedures: Familiarize yourself with airport emergency protocols.

Operational Restrictions
